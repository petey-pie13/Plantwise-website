About the Project

Empowering Smallholder Farmers

Empowering smallholder farmers in Kenya through data-driven advisory and diagnostic tools. PlantWise bridges the gap between scientific agricultural knowledge and the farmer on the ground — providing localized, accessible recommendations for crop selection and disease management.

Developed as part of a Computer Science initiative at Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST, 2026), this system targets the smallholder farming majority in Kenya's 47 counties.

Core Modules

What's Being Built

Two intelligent subsystems forming the backbone of PlantWise's decision support capabilities.

🌾 Ecological Crop Advisory

A fuzzy-logic algorithm that intelligently maps each farmer's county-level ecological data — including soil pH range, annual rainfall, altitude, and temperature — to the optimal staple crop from a database of 11 varieties. The system handles data imprecision gracefully, reflecting real-world agricultural variability.

Fuzzy Logic 11 Staple Crops County Mapping Soil pH Rainfall Data
🔬 Hybrid AI Disease Diagnosis

A two-layer diagnostic engine combining the Kindwise Plant.id API (cloud-based visual AI) with a locally hosted MySQL Knowledge Base of 50+ diseases and their localized, culturally appropriate treatments. Offline fallback ensures reliability in areas with limited connectivity.
